Ocala Equine Experience

Making your stay in Ocala feel like a vacation!

Rent, Ride, and Relax

At Ocala Equine Experience, we encourage you to experience all that Ocala has to offer in a quiet setting. The new World Equestrian Center is just across from the property. Downtown Ocala is a 10 minute drive for great shopping and restaurants.

Enjoy Ocala at its best and visit our unique facility.

  • Relax in one of four beautiful homes.
  • Comfort provided for your horses.
  • Large GGT Arena and Jump Field access.
  • Professional Facility Management.
  • Location! Location! Location!
